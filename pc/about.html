A bit about me...

Hey, nice to meet you!

I'm Alex, an Australian-American computer science student at the University of Melbourne!

I'm passionate about technology and its potential to change the world. I'm also a huge fan of design and architecture, which is why I created this website and embarked on the journey of trying to blend stunning architecture with sustainable living.

Having grown up in Paris, France, I take pride in my multi-cultural background and make use of it in my projects whenever appropriate.


On a side note, all of my projects are made by myself, primarily in Blender, and rendered using the Cycles render engine. This allows me to deliver exceptional work in record times, and to rapidly accomodate for the every-growing needs of my clients.
